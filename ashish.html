Late Night Love Poem

A Love Poem

After waiting for her all day,
she rushes out,unbothered,at almost 1am

In the hush of night interupted briefly
by the muffled mexican music from the
downstairs neighbor.

In the dark of night,lit up
by the orange glow of
our salt lamp on your side of the bed

Where i sleep or lay
or wait or write
late night love poems

When you are not here when
you are away and you say
you miss holding me

In your sleep and i say i miss that too
and we kiss goodnight over a screen,
across the miles

I roll over expecting sleep, i toss and turn waking
up with a poem instead scrawled out scribbled out
misspelled out

Onto the page because you know
it's course is not smooth,
it's nature is not boasting

It's not easy,love
is sometimes a parent,nurturing and
strong,love is sometimes a
toddler,learning and falling down

And i say remember let our love walk
with you in all that you do,

Love is both the pathway and the steady
light guiding us hand in hand,heart to heart,
home to each other
